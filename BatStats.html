Batting Statistics

Here, we're going to calculate some basic statistics, with regards to batters, and batting.

Strike Rate:

Strike rate is the scoring rate of an individual (via Wikipedia), and it is pretty simple. Take the runs scored by a batter, and divide it by the number of balls they have faced, then multiply it by 100.


Here's an example from the 2022 T20 World Cup semi-final played between England & India, Virat Kohli scored 50 runs in 40 balls. So, 50 runs divided by 40 balls, is 1.25, then multiply it by 100, that means he finished with a strike rate of 125.00.

Batting Average:

The batting average is how much a batter scores in one outing in terms of runs on average (via Wikipedia), and it gives an indication of the skill. Though as suggested by Wikipedia, and myself...do not make the sole metric!
