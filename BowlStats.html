Economy Rates

The economy is the average runs scored in a given over off a bowler (via Wikipedia), so if a bowler gives 24 runs across their 4 overs in a T20 spell, their economy is 6.00. The lower the better. However, one bad over can mess things up. Let's say a bowler in 3 overs has given 15 runs, their economy is 5.00, but if their 4th over goes for a whopping 20 runs, that means their 4 overs has given 35 runs, meaning an economy of 8.75.
