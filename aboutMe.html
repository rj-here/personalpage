About Me

Most of my projects have been in it, and I feel I've got a moderate command on it. Good or great would sound nice to say, but life humbles me, and I am reminded of much more progress is to be made, gradually over time. As mentioned in James Clear's Atomic Habits, just being 1% better daily is over 37 times better a year. Add to it, I've also been working on JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, in conjunction with GitHub, and building out my personal page here. I have always been fascinated in technology, particularly mainstream consumer electronics, like smartphones, tablets, computers, and headphones. Admitted I'm a little behind with a huge 50+ watch later list in YouTube, but I'll catch up over time. I've also been playing around with artificial intelligence models, mostly for fun. I am opening up to it though, using it to enhance my cover letter, but even then it is not directly copy pasted, because that is not sensible. GitHub Copilot is a bit too helpful...it suggests code & words which is very close if not exactly what I intended to type up, and it's impressive to see the progess of artificial intelligence, but this is what the threat is as well. Thankfully, I still know what it is doing, so I can explain the code and process upon being asked.
